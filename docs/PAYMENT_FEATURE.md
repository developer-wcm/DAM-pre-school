# Payment Feature Guide

## How to Use "Pay Outstanding"

### For Admin/Principal:

1. **Open Student Profile**
   - Go to Students tab
   - Click on any student

2. **Go to Fees Tab**
   - Toggle between "Monthly (12)" or "Quarterly (4)"
   - View unpaid fees

3. **Click "Pay Outstanding" Button**
   - Modal opens with all unpaid fees
   - All fees are auto-selected

4. **Select Fees to Pay**
   - Tap any fee to select/deselect
   - Can pay multiple fees at once

5. **Choose Payment Method**
   - Cash
   - UPI
   - Card
   - Cheque

6. **Enter Transaction ID** (optional)
   - For UPI/Card/Cheque only
   - Not required for Cash

7. **Click "Record Payment"**
   - Payment is saved to database
   - Fees marked as PAID
   - Stats update automatically

## Features:

✅ Multi-select fees
✅ 4 payment methods
✅ Transaction ID tracking
✅ Auto-updates stats
✅ Shows total amount
✅ Prevents duplicate payments
